    • About Us

    The Oaks are a mixed group of talented (and not so talented) ex-cricketers and first timers alike. Most members are affiliated with the official Oaks Cricket Club in some way. The age of our Players range from 18 to over 70 years. Grandsons have played with Grandfathers with little or no resulting arguments. Like all good Taverners we pride ourselves in our ability to "Talk a good game" without feeling the need to actually play a good one. We are a part of the Taverners league, a social cricket league with teams all over Dublin and beyond. This club is affiliated with the Oaks Cricket Club. If its the official club you were looking for, click here.

    Cricketing Info

    Taverners Cricket usually lasts around two and a half to three hours, with food and drinks afterwards. It is usually payed in the format of 15 overs of 8 balls or just a plain T20, rules and additional overs will be discussed between teams before play. Matches are usually played on a friday evening at 6 pm with the occasional practise sessions during the week. The Taverners season begins and ends at the same time of any Irish cricketing season, between the months of May and September. To be a part of the Taverners club will be 100 Euros for the season, with the occasional pitch maintenance costs needed to keep our grounds in pristene condition (usually around 25 Euros a year). Cricketing equiptment is provided.
