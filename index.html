Welcome to the Oaks

Welcome to the home of the Oaks Taverners Cricket Club, a social cricket club based in Knocklyon, Two Oaks Park. Residing in the outskirts of Dublin, we have the privilage of playing on one of the most the scenic grounds in Ireland. We are granted use of the grounds and facilities from the official Oaks Cricket Club who we show our up most grattitude, yet the Oaks Tavern is where it all started. We would love to invite you to share in the fun and love of the beautiful sport of Cricket!
